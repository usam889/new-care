Non-Emergency Medical Transportation

provides safe, reliable rides for individuals who need assistance getting to and from medical appointments, but do not require an ambulance. These services support seniors, patients with disabilities, and those without access to personal or public transportation.

Mobile Integrated Health (MIH)

(MIH) delivers healthcare services directly to patients in their homes or communities, reducing the need for emergency room visits. MIH connects patients with paramedics, nurses, or care teams to provide timely, non-emergency care and support.
